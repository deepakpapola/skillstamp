About this Course

What are the objectives of Interaction Designer Certification ?

Agenda of this course is provides an understand right process to design Learners will gain an understanding of what is involved in UX research, including conducting interviews, evaluating systems, and analyzing systems using principles of good design. Learners will also learn about the work involved in UX Design, including the generation of promising design solutions and the creation of prototypes at multiple levels of fidelity. By interleaving successive phases of UX Research and Design, learners will see how to learn from inevitable mistakes and improve towards a product with a great UX.

This course it the complete package of all you will learn more then other sources. All course are sync with each other. All learning contents had been designed by experts and current industries case studies.
